O nás


Since 2013, we have been providing professional services in the field of IT architecture and consulting. We specialize in the analysis, development, and integration of cloud systems to support business processes. Our goal is to design successful custom software solutions with emphasis on security and user-friendliness.

We also offer consultations in the field of cloud services design. We provide training in modern technologies and set up software development project management and organization processes. We help you apply, for example, the DevSecOps culture, agile processes, or the OWASP Application Security Verification Standard.

Our company is built on innovations and continuous learning. We avoid technical debt by regularly improving our approaches based on current state-of-the-art knowledge. Some members of our team pass on their experience as lecturers at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of West Bohemia in Pilsen.

Our workflows

We support you from the beginning till the end of the project and subsequently during its operation, maintenance, and further development. You are able to keep precise overview of our progress and project budget through regular updates in several-week long cycles.

Project initialization

    At the beginning, we..

  • establish a dedicated contact person, project analyst, and architect
  • set up mutual cooperation processes and communication channels

    The initial project phase usually consists of...

  • defining the project vision together
  • analysis of the project scope, schedule and costs estimation
  • setting up the development process - iterative with defined milestones, based on customer requirements

Requirements Analysis and Architectural Prototype

    Consists of...

  • analysis of business processes and main goals
  • familiarization with your existing environment
  • development of an architectural prototype

    Output

  • project´s architectural description in Archimate or UML
  • executable prototype with integrations into existing/planned environment

    Importance of developing an architectural prototype

  • verifies the feasibility of the proposed solution
  • uncovers any issues with integration into existing systems at an early stage of the project

Agile development process

    Regular iterations involve…

  • iteration planning
  • development and testing of the planned changes
  • presentation of the resulting increment to the customer

    Feedback

  • at this stage, a testing environment is available to the customer to control the gradual development of the project
  • the process allows for immediate responses to unexpected events, control of the project budget, and fluent change of priorities

Distribution, production operations, and support

    Operations and maintenance

  • operations in high-availability mode
  • monitoring 24/7 and automated backup processes
  • security updates and bug fixes
  • documentation, maintenance guide and transition to your ICT department

    Environments

  • our solutions can be operated in private infrastructure, public cloud, or hybrid mode
  • deployment processes are automated
  • we prefer making solutions without vendor lock-in

    Security

  • we apply the OWASP Application Security Verification Standard to our solutions
  • project is developed in accordance with your security policies and based on the current state-of-the-art knowledge
  • for critical projects or projects governing sensitive data, we recommend performing external security audit
